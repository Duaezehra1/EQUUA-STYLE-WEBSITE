Equua Style Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Welcome to the Equua Style FAQ section. Here, we address some commonly asked questions to provide you with quick and helpful information about our brand, products, and services.

What is Equua Style known for?

Equua Style is renowned for its unique blend of timeless elegance and cutting-edge innovation in the world of fashion. Our collections encompass a diverse range of styles, from classic pieces to products that integrate the latest technological advancements.

How can I purchase Equua Style products?

Equua Style products are available for purchase through our online boutique at www.equuastyle.com. Additionally, you can find our collections at our flagship stores, exclusive retail partners, and select pop-up shops. Use our Store Locator for more details.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we offer international shipping. During the checkout process on our online boutique, you can select your country, and shipping options and costs will be displayed.

What is your return policy?

Our return policy allows for returns within 30 days of the purchase date. Items must be in their original condition with tags attached. Visit our Returns and Exchanges page on the website for detailed instructions.
